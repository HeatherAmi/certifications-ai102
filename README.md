Hands-on projects and study materials for the **Microsoft AI-102: Designing and Implementing a Microsoft Azure AI Solution** certification.

AI-102 is the associate-level Azure AI certification. It covers planning, designing, and implementing Azure AI services — including Azure OpenAI, Cognitive Services, AI Search, Document Intelligence, and custom AI solutions. For Power Platform developers, this cert unlocks a much deeper understanding of how to integrate enterprise AI into Dataverse, model-driven apps, and custom connectors.

## Who this is for

- Anyone preparing for the AI-102 exam
- Power Platform developers wanting to build real AI-powered solutions
- Developers already holding PL-400 wanting to extend into Azure AI
- Anyone who has completed AI-900 and wants to go deeper

## What this repo will cover

- Planning and managing Azure AI solutions
- Azure OpenAI Service — deploying and working with models
- Azure AI Language, Speech, Vision, and Document Intelligence
- Azure AI Search and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) patterns
- Custom AI integration with Power Platform and Dataverse
- Practice projects connecting Azure AI services to the Power Platform

## How this connects to the rest of the series

AI-102 sits above [certifications-ai900](https://github.com/HeatherAmi/certifications-ai900) and alongside [certifications-pl400](https://github.com/HeatherAmi/certifications-pl400). The Azure Function and webhook patterns from PL-400 are directly applicable here.
